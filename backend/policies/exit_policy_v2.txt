2. Exit Policy
Probation Period: Standard 3 months (can vary by role).
Notice Period: 30 days during probation; 60 days after confirmation.
Resignation Process: Submit resignation via HRMS/email; serve notice period unless waived.
Handover: Mandatory knowledge transfer, completion of deliverables, and return of company assets (laptop, ID, etc.).
Full & Final Settlement: Processed within 30 days of last working day (salary dues, leave encashment, statutory payouts).
Relieving & Experience Letters: Issued after clearances within 7 working days.
Termination for Cause: Gross misconduct (fraud, data theft, harassment) can result in immediate termination after inquiry.
